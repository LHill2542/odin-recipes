Brownie Recipe

This easy brownie recipe takes about 30 minutes to make from start to finish for a chocolaty treat!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups White Sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 Cup Butter
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1/2 Cup Cocoa Powder
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 Cup Walnut Halves

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 Degrees F
  2. Grease a 9x13 inch pan
  3. Mix all ingredients besides walnuts in a large bowl until combined
  4. Spread batter into the prepared pan
  5. Decorate with walnut halves
  6. Bake until the top is dry and edges start to pull away from the edges of the pan, about 20-30 minutes
  7. Let cool before slicing into squares
  8. Enjoy!
